Stuff To Do

Here's a few of our favorite places to eat, drink, and hangout. If you're in town for a few days, check them out!

Brooklyn

  • Tacos el Bronco - Best tacos in Brooklyn, full stop. The red salsa is the best salsa.
  • Jpan - Friendly and delicious sushi place. Some house favorites are the Crazy Spicy Roll and the Winter Roll.
  • Naruto - Salty, spicy ramen. Not great for summer tho.
  • Tanoreen - Literally the best Middle Eastern food in New York City. You will not regret this trip to Bay Ridge. Recommend all the halloumi.
  • Brooklyn Bridge - A Bridge...partly in Brooklyn. Great views of downtown Brooklyn and Manhattan, and a tiny Lady Liberty.
  • Industry City Distillery - Local Sunset Park vodka distillery with killer Manhattan views from its tasting room.
  • The Levee - Basically a trash bar, but hosted a lot of venerated #TeamChrait history.
  • Prospect Park - Brooklyn's Central Park. It's pretty noice.

Manhattan

  • ABC Kitchen - Local and seasonal restaurant near Union Square. Go there and have a $200 lunch and you will not regret it even a little bit.
  • Vanessa's Dumplings - Suspiciously delicious dumplings given how cheap they are. A classic NYC spot.
  • AMNH - American Museum of Natural History. They've got dinoaurs and a plane'arium.
  • Statue of Liberty - If you're a descendent of immigrants, beware. It's a real tearjerker. Great place to get some perspective, if you need it.
  • Top of the Rock - Viewing deck for 30 Rockefeller Center that has great views of downtown, uptown, and Central Park.
  • High Line - An old elevated train line that is now a park on the westside. V neat.
  • Tenement Museum - A museum in an old apartment building that housed thousands of immigrants in the 19th-20th century.
  • Finn's - Bay Area sports bar. They don't show Raiders games, but whatever.

Get a Sweat Going

  • KORE New York - HIIT Studio that will destroy you.
  • Swerve - Competitive cycling studio.
  • Peloton - Bougie cycling studio with "really nice bikes" per Caitlin.
  • Row House - Pretty much what it sounds like. It's a rowing studio.
