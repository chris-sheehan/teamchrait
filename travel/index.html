Travel & Stay

Thanks for making the trip all the way to New York to celebrate with us (Editor's Note: if you're not coming out or live in NYC, please ignore the previous sentence). Below are a few tips to help on your trip and stay.

  • Getting Here / Airports

    • If you're flying in, both JFK and LaGuardia are convenient options. Flying into Newark is a little cheaper, but is not recommended as the commute is a lot longer and less convenient. In terms of getting from the airport, by public transit you can take the Air Train to the subway. Alternatively, yellow cabs from JFK to our neighborhood are ~$60; from LaGuardia they are ~$40.

  • Hotels / Airbnb

    • There's a hotel just around the block from the restaurant with good reviews, Hotel Le Bleu. Aside from that, there are always places on Airbnb that are both close and affordable.

  • Getting Around

    • If you're staying in Brooklyn or Manhattan, you won't need a vehicle. NYC has the greatest finest transportation in the world. In fact, New York City invented public transportation! We recommend downloading one of the many free subway map apps if you plan on exploring.
